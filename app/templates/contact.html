About

My name is Thibault Dody.

I am a lead engineer with a strong background in mathematics. My current work focuses on performing risk analysis for nuclear power plants, I also work on software development. We perform probability risk assessment related to external aggressions such as seismic, tornado missiles or floods.

I completed my MEng at MIT with a thesis focused on applying numerical optimization techniques to reduce the impact of earthquakes on buildings. Before joining the MIT program, I studied mathematics and physics as part of the preparatory program to enter French engineering schools, I then joined the MSc program at the ESTP school in Paris to study structural engineering with a strong emphasis on mathematics.

You can read more about my past projects or obtain a copy of my CV .
